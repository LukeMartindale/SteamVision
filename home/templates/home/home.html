Welcome

Welcome to Steam Vision where we try to make the decision of what games you play and purchase on Steam easier. We collect data from Steam on a daily basis, process it, and then make it avaliable for you to view. You can view the Reviews and player count as well as looking at the sentiment and prominent emotions are for the games you like or are interested in.

We collect data from Steam every day starting from 12AM over the next few hours, store, and process the data we collect fro you to view. Reviews are collected and processed every night, whilst player count is collected hourly.

Sentiment

On Steam reviews are rated on a simple voted up, voted down basis. Check out the sentiment for a game and its reviews to see just how positive or negative its reviews really are.
